11 All ages, <5 years to ≥60 years

Estimates in the all-ages category are provided where the widest possible age range in a study began at ≤2 years and ended at ≥60 years. Studies that exclusively recruited a specific age group are not included. Studies that only estimated VE for specific age groups are included if the age ranges studied started at <5 years and ended at >65 years.
